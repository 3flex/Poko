## 0.3.0
_2020-08-19_

## 0.2.3
_2020-05-04_

## 0.2.2
_2020-05-02_

Allow explicit `toString`, `equals`, and `hashCode` declarations in `@DataApi` classes. When one of
these is declared explicitly, that function will not be generated by Extra Care.

## 0.2.1
_2020-04-24_

## 0.2.0
_2020-04-21_

